Appol's Apple Pie

Hungry? Appol loves to turn his cousins into Apple pies.

Ingredients

  • 3 of Appol's cousins
  • 1 cup coconut flour
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp stevia (or any sweetener)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix coconut flour, cinnamon, and stevia until no lumps form.
  2. Pour into a pie mold and heat in the microwave for 1 minuite.
  3. Dice the apples. In a large bowl, add the diced apples and heat until they have a sauce-like consistency.
  4. Pour the sauce into the pie base. Serve and enjoy!
